Tarot Reading - About

“Tarot is a way of sorting out what's bothering you and getting advice from the best-informed source - you - in a way that you're likely to listen to.”
Rosemary Edghill, Speak Daggers to Her

The is a web application using Python, randomly selecting a card from a the 78 cards in the tarot deck.

The card images used are from the Rider Waite deck.

Descriptions and meanings are taken from Labyrinthos.
